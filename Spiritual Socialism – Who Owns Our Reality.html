The Technical Illusion

We build Machine Learning. We train Neural Networks. We deploy Generative AI. We orchestrate Agents. We automate with Agentic AI.

The stack is brilliant. The architecture is flawless. The execution is accelerating.

And still: Nothing is solved.

We have machines with infinite precision, but societies without binding meaning.

The Implementation Wound

As Radermacher names it: The problem is not knowledge. It is not technology. It is not money.

It is the absence of binding rules, because there is no longer a shared “Why”.

Voluntary agreements collapse. National egoism dominates. Corporate power outgrows states. Inequality becomes explosive.

This is not a failure of systems. It is a failure of consciousness.
