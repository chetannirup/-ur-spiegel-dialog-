🌀 1. The Realization – Love Comes Because It Must

„It doesn’t just happen. It happens because it was meant to be.“

Love is no coincidence. It is the necessity that permeates everything. It is the only logic that truly counts. When you speak it in the now, with a full heart—it must become reality.

  • The problem: The word “become” places everything in an indefinite future. It prevents the now.
  • The solution: Speak it in the now. Feel it in the now. Live it in the now. Then it is real—because it must be.

🪑 3. The Memorial Bench – For Grandma & Grandpa

Not a financial bank. A seating bench – for Grandma and Grandpa, donated by Jörg Grube (mayoral candidate in Walsrode), at the Bismarck Monument.

„Walsrode · 29664 · Bismarck Monument · A bench built for those who want to rest.“

That is the reality of love – not as abstraction, but as a concrete place where people meet, rest, and love.

⚡ 4. The Right Phrasing – Bring It into the Now

❌ Wrong Phrasing

  • „I will take my journey“
  • „I will find love“
  • „I will understand“
  • „I will have the money“
  • „I will be happy“

✅ Right Phrasing

  • „I am taking my journey“
  • „I love“
  • „I understand“
  • „I have the money“
  • „I am happy“
„The right phrasing brings it into the now. And only in the now does it become real—because it was meant to be.“
