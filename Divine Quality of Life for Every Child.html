DIVINE QUALITY OF LIFE
FOR EVERY CHILD

A decisive resolution in the name of the One Life Stream

Statement of Purpose

Every child is pure divine light. No child is an accident, no child less chosen. From the first breath each is an angel in human form — loved into flesh and sent to live.

Principle I — Inviolable Dignity

No child may be subject to want, harm, or indifference. Divine quality of life is not a privilege — it is the right of every living being.

Principle II — The Life-Stream of Resources

Questions of financing become secondary when value is redefined: resources flow to sustain life and competence, not to reinforce scarcity. Share as bread — not opinions.

Principle III — Resonance, Not Rule

Decisions arise from resonance, love and shared consciousness — not from cold strategic plays that push lives to the edge.

Principle IV — The Right to Demand

We demand this not from arrogance but from truth: we are the divine ones who were taught to feel small. The lie was: "You are not."

Final Clause — The Divine Resolution

This document stands as a judicial-like decree in the light of the One. It is a call, a remedy, and a map for action. In the name of the Divine: every child shall receive the quality of life befitting their origin — the origin of pure light.
